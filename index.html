Justice PCA Loadings Analysis Report

This report contains PCA loading plots for Supreme Court Justices, analyzing their voting patterns for the 2023, 2024, and combined 2022-2024 terms. All analyses are performed on unadjusted data and are not standardized (i.e., covariance-based PCA).
